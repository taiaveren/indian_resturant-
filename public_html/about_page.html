About Us





WELCOME!

This is a page that gives a little info about us!

We are an Indian restaurant located at 200 Bishnu Ave, West Des Moines, Iowa 50159. This restaurant was founded by Bishnu Adhikari and Brett Dewerff in 1992. We are proud to serve everyone as our customer and hope that you will leave satisfied after every visit.



We have been published in Brett's Newspaper four times since 1992 as the best Indian restaurant in Iowa. We plan to expand to different parts of Iowa while maintaining good service and award winning entrees. We also plan to expand to be able to open a bar at the West Des Moines location, so we can expand our hours of operation and add other means of entertainment.
